College FAQs

1. What courses does the college offer?
We offer UG, PG, and diploma courses in Arts, Science, and Commerce.
2. How do I apply for admission?
Visit the Admissions page on our website and follow the application steps.
3. What is the fee structure?
Fee details vary by program and can be found in the admissions brochure.
4. Does the college provide hostel facilities?
Yes, separate hostels are available for boys and girls with basic amenities.
5. What is the college's placement record?
We have a strong placement cell and maintain over 80% placement rate annually.
6. Are there any scholarships available?
Yes, we provide scholarships based on merit and financial need.
7. Is there a dress code in the college?
Yes, students are expected to wear the prescribed uniform on working days.
8. What extracurricular activities are available?
We have sports, NSS, cultural clubs, and technical societies to engage students.
